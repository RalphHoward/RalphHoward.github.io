    Jupyter Notebooks:
    If you have Jupyter installed on your computer these notebooks can be down loaded and run.
  • plot_from_curvature.ipynb This notebook plots the curve given the curvature function.
  • plot_and_find_curvature.ipynb This notebook takes the parametric form of a planar curve, plots it and computes the unit tangent, unit normal, and the curvature.
  • polar_curvature.ipynb This notebook plots the graph of a curve given in polar coordinates as r = f(theta) and computes its curvature.
  • evolute.ipynbThis notebook plots a curve together with its evolute.
  • plot3d_curve_find_curvature_torsion.ipynb A notebook that plots a 3D curve given parametrically, and computes its Frenet frame, curvature, and torsion.
