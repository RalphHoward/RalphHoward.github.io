Algebra Exams.

The current algebra qualifying exams are based on the Math 701 and Math 702 classes. Earlier the exams were based on Math 700 (linear algebra) and Math 701. This is why the list of algebra exams here only goes back to 2010. George McNulty made a list of algebra questions from the earlier exams sorted by subject: group theory, ring theory, and linear algebra.
