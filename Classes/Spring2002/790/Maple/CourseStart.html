Maple
Training Services

This Tutorial has been designed by the Mathematics Department at Seattle Central Community College, to give you a complete, self-paced and hands-on introduction to the basic commands that you will need to use Maple effectively.

The six sections of the tutorial will step you through each topic with many opportunities for you to practice and experiment with Maple. 

If you would like more information about this tutorial, please contact Mike Pepe, mipepe@sccd.ctc.edu.

You must have Maple V or Maple 6 installed on your computer to open the worksheets listed below. If you don't, you may request a Free Maple 6 Trial CD.
