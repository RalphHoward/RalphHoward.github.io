Mathematics 241 is listed as a prerequisite to this class. While it will not be assumed that you remember everything from that class, it will be assumed that you do remember the basics about vectors and how to work with them. This includes adding, subtracting, and multiplying them by scalars. Also the dot product and the cross product and what they mean geometrically and how to use them to solve geometric problems such as finding the distance from a point to a plane.
