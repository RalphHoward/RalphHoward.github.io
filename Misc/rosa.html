The Rosa Maria Garcia Memorial Page

The formidable lass above and frolicking with me below is the late and much lamented Miss Rosa Maria Garcia with whom I lived from 1977 to 1982. Of Latin American heritage, being a spectacled caiman (Caiman crocodilus crocodilus), she was a very important part of the social life of North Hollywood CA during her time there. Her many friends still miss her. I take some comfort in knowing that when she went west part of me went with her.
